Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Salary Clock. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection: "Local-First" Approach

We believe that your data belongs to you. To ensure the highest level of privacy:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our own servers. This includes your name, contact details, or financial records entered into the app.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track your behavior or collect data for profiling.

3. Data Storage and iCloud

  • On-Device Storage: All data you input (such as hourly rates, work logs, and emotional journals) is stored exclusively on your device.
  • iCloud Backup: If you have enabled iCloud Backup on your iOS device, your app data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account managed by Apple. We do not have access to this data; it is encrypted and protected by Apple’s privacy infrastructure.

4. Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device (except for your own iCloud backups), the security of your information depends on your device's security features, such as FaceID, TouchID, or Passcode. We recommend keeping your iOS version up to date to ensure the latest security patches.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we cannot delete it for you. You have full control:

  • To delete all data, simply uninstall the application from your device.
  • To remove data from iCloud, you can manage your storage in your iOS System Settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
